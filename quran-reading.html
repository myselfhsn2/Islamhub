Quran Reading Course

Master the correct pronunciation and recitation of the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed rules

About This Course

The Quran Reading course at IslamHub.org is designed to help students of all ages learn how to read the Holy Quran correctly with proper pronunciation (Makhārij al-Ḥurūf) and Tajweed rules. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to improve your recitation, our experienced teachers will guide you through a structured curriculum tailored to your needs.

Our one-on-one classes ensure that you receive personalized attention and can progress at your own pace. Each session is interactive, allowing for real-time feedback and corrections from your teacher.

Course Curriculum

Level 1: Qaidah (Basics)

  • Introduction to Arabic alphabet
  • Recognition and pronunciation of letters
  • Vowel marks (Fatha, Kasra, Damma)
  • Reading simple letter combinations
  • Sukun and Shaddah rules

Level 2: Basic Tajweed Rules

  • Correct points of articulation (Makhārij)
  • Understanding Ghunnah (nasalization)
  • Rules of Noon Saakin and Tanween
  • Rules of Meem Saakin
  • Practice with short Surahs

Level 3: Advanced Tajweed

  • Raa rules
  • Al-Qalqalah (echoing)
  • Al-Madd (prolongation) rules
  • Waqf (stopping) rules
  • Fluent recitation with proper rhythm

Level 4: Mastery and Certification

  • Recitation of longer Surahs
  • Focus on recitation style (tarteel)
  • Comprehensive revision of all Tajweed rules
  • Final assessment and evaluation
  • Certification of completion
