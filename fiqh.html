Islamic Studies: Basic Fiqh

Master the fundamentals of Islamic jurisprudence through structured, easy-to-understand lessons

Course Overview

Our Basic Fiqh course is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of Islamic jurisprudence in a structured and accessible manner. Led by qualified scholars, this course covers essential aspects of daily Islamic practices and their underlying principles.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of Islamic jurisprudence and its importance
  • Detailed understanding of daily worship acts
  • Practical application of Fiqh rules in modern context
  • Common questions and their scholarly answers

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Fiqh

  • Understanding what is Fiqh
  • Sources of Islamic Law
  • Schools of Thought in Fiqh
  • Importance of Fiqh in daily life

Module 2: Purification (Taharah)

  • Types of water and their rulings
  • Wudu and its proper method
  • Ghusl and its requirements
  • Tayammum and its conditions

Module 3: Prayer (Salah)

  • Conditions and pillars of prayer
  • Different types of prayers
  • Common mistakes in prayer
  • Making up missed prayers

Module 4: Zakat and Fasting

  • Understanding Zakat calculation
  • Rules of Ramadan fasting
  • Voluntary charity and fasting
  • Modern applications of Zakat
